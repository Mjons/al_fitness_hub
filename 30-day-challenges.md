# 30-Day Pillar Challenges

Each pillar has a progressive 30-day challenge. Tasks evolve weekly, building from 1 task in Week 1 to 4 tasks by Week 4.

---

## 1. BREATHING PILLAR

### Week 1 (Days 1-7) - 1 Task
- [ ] **Morning Breath Check** - Take 5 deep belly breaths upon waking

### Week 2 (Days 8-14) - 2 Tasks
- [ ] **Morning Breath Check** - Take 5 deep belly breaths upon waking
- [ ] **Stress Reset** - 4-7-8 breathing when stressed (inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s)

### Week 3 (Days 15-21) - 3 Tasks
- [ ] **Morning Breath Check** - Take 5 deep belly breaths upon waking
- [ ] **Stress Reset** - 4-7-8 breathing when stressed
- [ ] **Box Breathing Break** - 5-minute box breathing during lunch (4s inhale, 4s hold, 4s exhale, 4s hold)

### Week 4 (Days 22-30) - 4 Tasks
- [ ] **Morning Breath Check** - Take 5 deep belly breaths upon waking
- [ ] **Stress Reset** - 4-7-8 breathing when stressed
- [ ] **Box Breathing Break** - 5-minute box breathing during lunch
- [ ] **Evening Wind-Down** - 10 slow breaths before bed, extending exhale

## 2. SLEEP PILLAR

### Week 1 (Days 1-7) - 1 Task
- [ ] **Consistent Wake Time** - Wake up at the same time every day (within 30 min)

### Week 2 (Days 8-14) - 2 Tasks
- [ ] **Consistent Wake Time** - Wake up at the same time every day
- [ ] **Screen Curfew** - No screens 30 minutes before bed

### Week 3 (Days 15-21) - 3 Tasks
- [ ] **Consistent Wake Time** - Wake up at the same time every day
- [ ] **Screen Curfew** - No screens 30 minutes before bed
- [ ] **Cool & Dark** - Bedroom temp below 68°F, blackout conditions

### Week 4 (Days 22-30) - 4 Tasks
- [ ] **Consistent Wake Time** - Wake up at the same time every day
- [ ] **Screen Curfew** - No screens 30 minutes before bed
- [ ] **Cool & Dark** - Bedroom temp below 68°F, blackout conditions
- [ ] **Wind-Down Ritual** - 15-min relaxation routine (reading, stretching, journaling)

## 3. HYDRATION PILLAR

### Week 1 (Days 1-7) - 1 Task
- [ ] **Morning Hydration** - Drink 16oz water within 30 min of waking

### Week 2 (Days 8-14) - 2 Tasks
- [ ] **Morning Hydration** - Drink 16oz water within 30 min of waking
- [ ] **Water Tracking** - Log and drink at least 64oz total daily

### Week 3 (Days 15-21) - 3 Tasks
- [ ] **Morning Hydration** - Drink 16oz water within 30 min of waking
- [ ] **Water Tracking** - Log and drink at least 64oz total daily
- [ ] **Electrolyte Balance** - Add electrolytes or mineral-rich water once daily

### Week 4 (Days 22-30) - 4 Tasks
- [ ] **Morning Hydration** - Drink 16oz water within 30 min of waking
- [ ] **Water Tracking** - Log and drink at least 64oz total daily
- [ ] **Electrolyte Balance** - Add electrolytes or mineral-rich water once daily
- [ ] **Pre-Meal Hydration** - Drink 8oz water 15 min before each meal

### Week 2 (Days 8-14) - 2 Tasks

### Week 3 (Days 15-21) - 3 Tasks

### Week 4 (Days 22-30) - 4 Tasks

## 5. MOVEMENT PILLAR

### Week 1 (Days 1-7) - 1 Task
- [ ] **Daily Walk** - Walk for at least 10 minutes outdoors

### Week 2 (Days 8-14) - 2 Tasks
- [ ] **Daily Walk** - Walk for at least 15 minutes outdoors
- [ ] **Movement Snacks** - 3 micro-movements throughout the day (squats, stretches, stairs)

### Week 3 (Days 15-21) - 3 Tasks
- [ ] **Daily Walk** - Walk for at least 20 minutes outdoors
- [ ] **Movement Snacks** - 3 micro-movements throughout the day
- [ ] **Strength Session** - 2x per week bodyweight or resistance training (15+ min)

### Week 4 (Days 22-30) - 4 Tasks
- [ ] **Daily Walk** - Walk for at least 20 minutes outdoors
- [ ] **Movement Snacks** - 3 micro-movements throughout the day
- [ ] **Strength Session** - 2x per week bodyweight or resistance training
- [ ] **Mobility Work** - 5-10 min daily stretching or yoga

### Week 3 (Days 15-21) - 3 Tasks

## 7. MINDFULNESS PILLAR

### Week 1 (Days 1-7) - 1 Task
- [ ] **Gratitude Moment** - Write or think of 3 things you're grateful for

### Week 2 (Days 8-14) - 2 Tasks
- [ ] **Gratitude Moment** - Write or think of 3 things you're grateful for
- [ ] **Mindful Minute** - 1 minute of present-moment awareness (focus on breath or senses)

### Week 3 (Days 15-21) - 3 Tasks
- [ ] **Gratitude Moment** - Write or think of 3 things you're grateful for
- [ ] **Mindful Minute** - Extend to 5 minutes of meditation or mindfulness
- [ ] **Reflection Check** - End-of-day reflection: What went well? What to improve?

### Week 4 (Days 22-30) - 4 Tasks
- [ ] **Gratitude Moment** - Write or think of 3 things you're grateful for
- [ ] **Mindful Minute** - 5-10 minutes of meditation or mindfulness
- [ ] **Reflection Check** - End-of-day reflection journal
- [ ] **Intention Setting** - Set one clear intention each morning

---

## Summary: Task Progression

| Week | Tasks | Focus |
|------|-------|-------|
| 1 | 1 task | Foundation - build the core habit |
| 2 | 2 tasks | Expansion - add complementary habit |
| 3 | 3 tasks | Deepening - increase complexity |
| 4 | 4 tasks | Mastery - full daily practice |

- Progress ring showing % completion
- Streak counter with fire icon
- End-of-challenge summary and badge

- Day streak bonuses
- Weekly milestone badges

- Cross-pillar achievements for completing multiple challenges
