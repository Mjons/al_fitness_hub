# 21-Day Pillar Challenges

Each pillar has a progressive 21-day challenge designed to build lasting habits through 4 phases + a reward day.

---

## Challenge Structure

| Phase | Days | Tasks | Trigger |
|-------|------|-------|---------|
| **Phase 1** | 1-5 | 1 task | Encouragement message for the pillar |
| **Phase 2** | 6-10 | 2 tasks | Pre-recorded video (Day 10) |
| **Phase 3** | 11-15 | 3 tasks | 15% discount coupon |
| **Phase 4** | 16-20 | 4 tasks | Final push to completion |
| **Day 21** | 21 | Celebration | Cherry on top reward |

### Day 21 Reward (Cherry on Top)
Completing the 21-day challenge unlocks:
- **Free Behavior Framework Call with Coach Al** - Personalized strategy session
- **Free 1-Hour Training Session with Al** - In-person or virtual workout

*These rewards incentivize users to complete the challenge AND take the next step in their journey.*

### Day 21 "Cherry on Top" Challenges (Per Pillar)
Each pillar has a unique Day 21 capstone challenge - a special experience that celebrates their transformation and creates momentum toward booking their free call.

| Pillar | Cherry on Top Challenge | Why It Works |
|--------|------------------------|--------------|
| Breathing | **The 20-Minute Breath Journey** | Extended breathwork creates a profound state shift they'll want to explore further |
| Sleep | **The Sleep Sanctuary Reset** | Transforming their space makes the habit tangible and permanent |
| Hydration | **The Hydration Awareness Day** | Full-day tracking reveals how far they've come |
| Nutrition | **The Celebration Meal** | Cooking a nourishing meal connects emotion to nutrition |
| Movement | **The Personal Record Day** | Achieving a PR creates confidence to push further with coaching |
| Environment | **The Digital Sunset** | A full evening unplugged shows them what's possible |
| Mindfulness | **The Silent Hour** | Extended mindfulness opens the door to deeper practice |

## 1. BREATHING PILLAR

### Phase 1 (Days 1-5) - 1 Task
- [ ] **Morning Breath Check** - Take 5 deep belly breaths upon waking

### Phase 2 (Days 6-10) - 2 Tasks
- [ ] **Morning Breath Check** - Take 5 deep belly breaths upon waking
- [ ] **Stress Reset** - 4-7-8 breathing when stressed (inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s)

### Phase 3 (Days 11-15) - 3 Tasks
- [ ] **Morning Breath Check** - Take 5 deep belly breaths upon waking
- [ ] **Stress Reset** - 4-7-8 breathing when stressed
- [ ] **Box Breathing Break** - 5-minute box breathing during lunch (4s inhale, 4s hold, 4s exhale, 4s hold)

### Phase 4 (Days 16-20) - 4 Tasks
- [ ] **Morning Breath Check** - Take 5 deep belly breaths upon waking
- [ ] **Stress Reset** - 4-7-8 breathing when stressed
- [ ] **Box Breathing Break** - 5-minute box breathing during lunch
- [ ] **Evening Wind-Down** - 10 slow breaths before bed, extending exhale

### Day 21 - Celebration
Complete all 4 tasks + unlock your rewards!

- Show locked tasks as grayed out with "Unlocks Day X"

- Streak counter with fire icon
- Day 21 special celebration animation

- Day streak bonuses
- Phase completion badges (4 phases)
