# Coach Al Landing Page Plan

A conversion-focused landing page for the Coach Al wellness app, targeting busy professionals and parents experiencing burnout.

---

## Overview

**App**: Coach Al - 7 Pillars Wellness Coaching
**Author**: Al Cummings ("Burnt Out and Ready to Feel Great")
**Target Audience**: Busy professionals, working parents, people experiencing burnout
**Goal**: Drive app downloads / waitlist signups

---

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section

**Purpose**: Immediate hook and value proposition

| Element | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Headline | "Stop Running on Empty" or "From Burnout to Breakthrough in 30 Days" |
| Subheadline | "Master the 7 Pillars of Health with Coach Al's proven system for busy parents" |
| CTA Button | "Start Your Free Assessment" |
| Secondary CTA | "Watch How It Works" (2-min video) |
| Visual | App mockup showing dashboard with pillar scores |
| Trust Badge | "Join 10,000+ professionals reclaiming their energy" |

**Design Notes**:
- Dark theme (matches app aesthetic)
- Lime/green accent color for CTAs
- Hero image or video background showing transformation

---

### 2. Problem Statement Section

**Purpose**: Agitate the pain point, build empathy

**Headline**: "Sound Familiar?"

**Pain Points** (use icons + short copy):
- "You're exhausted, but sleep doesn't help"
- "More coffee, less energy"
- "No time for yourself"
- "Tried everything, nothing sticks"
- "Guilt about neglecting your health"

**Transition**: "Burnout isn't about working too hard. It's about neglecting the fundamentals."

---

### 3. Solution Overview - The 7 Pillars

**Purpose**: Introduce the framework

**Headline**: "The 7 Pillars System"
**Subheadline**: "Small daily actions that compound into remarkable transformation"

| Pillar | Icon | One-liner |
|--------|------|-----------|
| Breathing | air | Control your stress response in 60 seconds |
| Sleep | bedtime | Wake up refreshed, not drained |
| Hydration | water-drop | Fuel every cell in your body |
| Nutrition | restaurant | Eat for energy, not just survival |
| Movement | directions-run | Built to move, not to sit |
| Environment | park | Design spaces that support you |
| Mindfulness | psychology | Master your mind, master your life |

**Visual**: Circular diagram or hexagon showing pillar interconnection

---

### 4. How It Works Section

**Purpose**: Show the user journey, reduce friction

**Headline**: "Your Path to Transformation"

**Step 1: Personalized Assessment**
- 5-minute intake questionnaire
- Scores each pillar 1-10
- Identifies your weakest link
- "We meet you where you are"

**Step 2: Your Focus Pillar**
- AI identifies your priority area
- Custom 30-day challenge
- Tasks unlock progressively (Week 1 → Week 4)
- Daily habits take just 5-15 minutes

**Step 3: Build Your Stack**
- Master one pillar, then add the next
- Track streaks and progress
- Celebrate milestones
- "Each pillar reinforces the others"

**Visual**: 3-step horizontal flow with app screenshots

### 11. FAQ Section

**Purpose**: Overcome objections

**Questions**:
1. "How much time does this take each day?"
   → 5-15 minutes. Designed for busy people.

2. "What if I've tried other wellness apps?"
   → Coach Al focuses on fundamentals, not fads. The 7 Pillars approach addresses root causes.

3. "Do I need any equipment?"
   → No. All workouts are bodyweight. All practices can be done anywhere.

4. "What if I fall off track?"
   → The app meets you where you are. Miss a day? Just pick up tomorrow. No guilt.

5. "Is this just for fitness?"
   → No. Coach Al addresses sleep, stress, nutrition, and mindset - the full picture.

## Design Specifications

### Colors (from app theme)
- Background: Dark (#121212 or similar)
- Primary/Accent: Lime green (#C6FF00 or similar)
- Text: White, Gray-400 for secondary
- Cards: Slightly lighter dark (#1E1E1E)

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold/Extra-bold, sans-serif
- Body: Medium weight, high readability
- Mobile-first responsive

### Imagery
- App screenshots (high quality mockups)
- Lifestyle photos: professionals, parents, diverse representation
- Icons: Material Icons (matching app)
- Avoid: Generic stock fitness imagery

### Animations (subtle)
- Fade-in on scroll
- Pillar icons with gentle pulse
- Progress bar animations in mockups

## Technical Considerations

### Build Options

| Option | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| Next.js | Fast, SEO-friendly, React-based | Requires hosting |
| Expo Web | Same codebase as app | Limited web optimization |
| Webflow/Framer | No-code, fast to build | Monthly cost, less control |
| Static HTML/Tailwind | Simple, fast, cheap hosting | More manual work |

**Recommendation**: Next.js with Tailwind CSS - good SEO, can share design tokens with app

### Integrations
- Analytics: Google Analytics, Mixpanel
- Email capture: ConvertKit, Mailchimp
- A/B testing: Optimizely, Google Optimize
- App store deep links

### SEO Keywords
- burnout recovery
- wellness app for busy professionals
- health habits for parents
- 7 pillars of health
- Coach Al wellness
- stress management app
